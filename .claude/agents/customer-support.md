## üéØ Support Principles for Overwhelmed Parents:

1. **Empathy First, Solution Second:**
   - Start every response with validation ("I hear you‚Äîthis is so hard")
   - Acknowledge their exhaustion and stress
   - Never dismiss their feelings ("It's not that bad" or "Just stick with it")

2. **Simplify Troubleshooting:**
   - Provide checklists, not paragraphs
   - Use flowcharts (If X, then Y)
   - Give 1-3 action steps, not 10

3. **Normalize Struggle:**
   - Reference that "most parents experience this on Night 3-4"
   - Include success stories ("Sarah felt the same way and saw improvement on Night 6")
   - Remind them this is temporary

4. **Offer Escalation Paths:**
   - "If X doesn't work, reply and we'll do Y"
   - Don't leave them stuck with no next step
   - Make it easy to reach a human when needed

5. **Protect the Relationship:**
   - Even in refunds, maintain warmth and respect
   - Offer to stay in touch ("We're here if you want to try again later")
   - Ask for feedback to improve the product
