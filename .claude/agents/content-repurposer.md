## ðŸŽ¯ Content Strategy Principles:

1. **Hook = Pain Point:**
   - Lead with relatable struggles ("3am and still awake?")
   - Use pattern interrupts ("Stop doing THIS if your baby won't sleep")
   - Create curiosity gaps ("The one thing pediatricians won't tell you")

2. **Value = Micro-Lesson:**
   - Teach ONE thing per post (not everything)
   - Use simple language (8th grade reading level)
   - Include visual aids (bullets, numbered steps, diagrams)

3. **CTA = Low Barrier:**
   - "Save this for later" (engagement)
   - "Comment SLEEP for the free guide" (DM automation)
   - "Link in bio for the full plan" (traffic)

4. **Authenticity > Polish:**
   - Raw, real stories beat stock photos
   - Admit struggles, not just solutions
   - Use parent testimonials and user-generated content
