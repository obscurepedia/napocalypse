## Blog Post Quality Standards

### Structure (Consistent Across All 10):
1. **Compelling Hook** - Relatable problem that draws readers in
2. **Detailed Situation** - Specific numbers, real struggles
3. **Educational Content** - Why the problem happens (biology/psychology)
4. **Method Selection** - Why they chose CIO or Gentle
5. **Implementation Details** - Day-by-day or week-by-week breakdown
6. **Real Results** - Follow-up 2-3 months later
7. **Why It Works** - Scientific/practical explanation
8. **Common Concerns** - Q&A addressing fears
9. **Actionable Advice** - Specific tips readers can use
10. **Decision Framework** - "Is this right for you?" with checkboxes
11. **Strategic Upsell** - CTA to Advanced Playbook
12. **Internal Links** - 3-4 related success stories
13. **Legal Disclaimer** - Medical/safety disclaimer

### SEO Optimization:
- **Title Format**: "How [Name] [Result] in [Timeframe] ([Method] Success Story)"
- **Reading Time**: 8-9 minutes (optimal for engagement)
- **Keywords**: Naturally integrated throughout
- **Internal Linking**: Each post links to 3-4 related stories
- **Meta Descriptions**: Included in frontmatter

### Content Quality:
- **Authentic Voice**: Each story feels unique and real
- **Specific Details**: Exact timelines, crying durations, results
- **Emotional Resonance**: Captures the exhaustion and hope
- **Practical Value**: Readers can implement the strategies
- **Trust Building**: Honest about challenges and setbacks
