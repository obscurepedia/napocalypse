## Blog Post Structure (Consistent Across All 10)

### Standard Sections:
1. **Opening Hook** - Relatable problem statement
2. **The Situation** - Detailed description of the challenge
3. **Why This Problem Happens** - Educational content explaining the biology/psychology
4. **Why They Chose This Method** - Decision-making process
5. **The Implementation Plan** - Week-by-week or day-by-day breakdown
6. **The Results** - Follow-up after 2-3 months
7. **Why This Method Works** - Educational explanation
8. **Common Concerns** - Q&A format
9. **Advice for Parents** - Actionable tips
10. **Is This Right for You?** - Decision framework with checkboxes
11. **CTA to Playbook** - Upsell to Advanced Playbook
12. **Related Stories** - Internal links to other blog posts
13. **Disclaimer** - Medical/legal disclaimer

### SEO Optimization:
- **Title Format**: "How [Name] [Achieved Result] in [Timeframe] ([Method] Success Story)"
- **Meta Description**: Included in frontmatter
- **Reading Time**: 8-9 minutes
- **Internal Linking**: Each post links to 3-4 related stories
- **Keywords**: Naturally integrated (sleep training, CIO, gentle methods, etc.)
