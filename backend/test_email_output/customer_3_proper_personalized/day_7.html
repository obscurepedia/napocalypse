Day 7: Finding the Wins

Hi Test Customer,

You've been at this for a full week.

I know you're probably exhausted. I know you might be wondering if it's working. I know you're looking at other parents whose babies "just sleep naturally" and questioning everything.

Here's what I need you to do: Stop comparing. Start celebrating.

Progress is not a straight line. It's messy, uneven, two-steps-forward-one-step-back. And it's easy to miss if you're only looking for "perfect."

Today, we're finding the wins.

What Progress Looks Like for You

{wins_challenge_block}

Your Task for Today: Document Your Win (3 Minutes)

Step 1: Open your phone's Notes app (or grab a piece of paper and pen)

Step 2: Write "WEEK 1 WIN:" at the top

Step 3: Complete this sentence - choose the one that fits best:

Progress on crying: "My baby's crying decreased from ____ minutes on Night 1 to ____ minutes on Night 6."

Progress on sleep stretches: "My longest sleep stretch went from ____ hours to ____ hours."

Progress on my confidence: "On Night 1, I felt ____. Now on Night 6, I feel ____."

Progress on independence: "My baby went from needing ____ to fall asleep to now needing ____."

Progress on consistency: "I successfully stuck with my Gentle method for ____ nights in a row, even when it was hard."

Can't pick just one? Write them all. You deserve to see every bit of progress.

Step 4: Make it visible

Choose one place you'll see this win every day:

  • Phone lock screen: Take a screenshot of your note, set as wallpaper
  • Bathroom mirror: Write it on a sticky note, stick to mirror
  • Fridge: Write on paper, use magnet to post it
  • Nightstand: Keep the note next to your bed where you'll see it during night wakings

Why this matters: On rough nights (and they'll still happen), this note is your proof that you're making progress. It's not just "feeling" better - it's measurable, real change. And you did that.

These small wins are the foundation of lasting change. You're building healthier sleep habits one night at a time.
