Today's Goal: The 3-Step Routine

A bedtime routine signals to your baby's brain: "Sleep is coming." The key is doing the same 3 things, in the same order, every single night.

Here's a classic routine for your 7-12 month old:

  1. Bath or Wipe-Down - A warm bath is soothing. Don't have time? A quick wipe with a warm cloth works just fine.
  2. PJs & Story - Get into pajamas, then read one (and only one!) short, quiet book with dim lights.
  3. Brief Cuddle - End with a brief cuddle and say the same phrase every night: "I love you, it's time to sleep."

Your task tonight - pick your 3 steps:

Choose exactly 3 steps from this list for your 7-12 month old. Yes, just 3. You can adjust later, but tonight commit to these 3 in order:

Mix & Match Options (Babies):

  • Bath or warm washcloth wipe-down - 5 minutes
  • Gentle massage with lotion - 3-5 minutes
  • Get into pajamas - 2 minutes
  • Read 1 short book (yes, they're listening even if they wiggle!) - 5 minutes
  • Sing a lullaby or play white noise - 2-3 minutes
  • Final feeding (if still feeding before bed) - 10 minutes
  • Brief cuddle + goodnight phrase - 2 minutes

Example routines that work:

  • Quick (15 min): Bath ‚Üí Pajamas ‚Üí Brief cuddle ‚Üí Bed
  • With feeding (18 min): Pajamas ‚Üí Feed ‚Üí Lullaby ‚Üí Bed
  • Soothing (20 min): Bath ‚Üí Massage ‚Üí Pajamas ‚Üí Cuddle ‚Üí Bed

Do this tonight:

  • Write down your 3 steps (in your phone or on paper)
  • Do them in the same order tonight
  • Keep the total routine to 15-20 minutes max
  • Repeat the exact same routine tomorrow night

Important: The routine doesn't put your baby to sleep - it just signals that sleep is coming. They still need to fall asleep on their own.

The predictability is what creates the magic. Within a week, your baby will start recognizing "Oh, this means sleep time."
