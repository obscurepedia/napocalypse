You're on a roll! You've observed the old routine and optimized the sleep space. Today, we'll create a new, predictable bedtime routine that will become a powerful sleep cue for your baby.

A good routine doesn't need to be long or complicated. The goal is consistency, not complexity. It should be a 15-20 minute wind-down period that you can do every single night.

Your Goal for Today: The 3-Step Routine

Choose three simple, calming activities to do in order before bed. Here is a classic example:

  1. Bath or Wipe-Down: A warm bath or a quick wipe with a warm cloth can be very soothing.
  2. PJs & Story: Get into pajamas, and then read one (and only one!) short, boring book in a dim light.
  3. Feed & Cuddle: If a feed is part of your routine, do it now. Keep it calm and quiet. End with a brief cuddle and a consistent phrase like, "I love you, it's time to sleep."

Your Task: Decide on your 3 steps. Perform them in the same order tonight, and every night from now on. The predictability is what creates the magic.

This routine will soon be your baby's strongest signal that sleep is coming.
