üåü Rachel's Story: Breaking Feed-to-Sleep Gently

The Challenge:

Baby: Ethan, 8 months old
Problem: Would only sleep while nursing, waking 6-8 times per night
Parents' goal: Break the association without cry-it-out

"I couldn't do cry-it-out. It didn't align with my parenting values. I needed a gentler approach, even if it took longer."

The Gentle Solution:

Rachel used gradual weaning and the "pantley pull-off" technique:

  • Week 1: Separated nursing from falling asleep at bedtime
  • Week 2: Used pull-off technique to reduce nursing duration
  • Week 3: Gradual night weaning of non-hunger sessions
  • Final result: Sleeping 11+ hours with one feed
Final Results (3 weeks):
From 8 nursing sessions ‚Üí 1 feeding
From 2-3 hours of sleep ‚Üí 7+ hours
